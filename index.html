Meet Taylor

Registered Dietitian & Endurance Athlete

Hi, I'm Taylor Brower, a Registered Dietitian and endurance athlete. I founded Tune Up Nutrition because I saw too many cyclists and endurance athletes struggling with the same problems: bonking on long rides, under-fueling during training blocks, and feeling uncertain about what to eat before, during, and after their workouts.

I've been there myself and I know firsthand how frustrating it is to pour hours into training, only to have your nutrition hold you back.

Cyclists know the value of a good bike tune-up. You can have the best frame and the strongest legs, but if your gears are grinding or your brakes aren't aligned, your ride suffers. Nutrition is the same way: it's often the missing piece holding athletes back. Tune Up Nutrition is here to make sure your fueling is as dialed as your training plan and your bike setup.

Values

  • Performance without extremes
    No fad diets, no under-fueling, no gimmicks.
  • Empowerment through education
    Athletes leave with knowledge and confidence, not dependence.
  • Sustainability
    Plans fit into real life, not just race day.
  • Inclusivity
    Open to athletes of all levels, backgrounds, and body types.

Who I Help

I specialize in working with:

  • Cyclists training for centuries, fondos, gravel races, and stage races
  • Athletes who feel lost about what/when/how much to eat
  • Runners and multisport athletes who want to optimize fueling
  • Athletes who want to stop bonking, cramping, or fading late in events
